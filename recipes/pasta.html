Must have ingredients list (4 servings)

  • 4 cup pasta penne
  • 2 litre water
  • 4 tablespoon virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • black pepper as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 4 large tomato
  • salt as required
  • 4 teaspoon chilli flakes

    • For Garnishing

  • Grandma's favorite Basil leaves (6 leaves)

How to make Arrabbiata Pasta

  1. Blend chopped tomatoes for tomato puree and saut√© garlic

    2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium flame and add chopped garlic in it, saute till the raw smell of garlic goes away. When done, add red chilli flakes in the pan and saute for another few seconds. Meanwhile, blend chopped tomato into a puree in a blender jar.

  2. Prepare tomato-garlic puree

    3. Now, add tomato puree to the pan, mix well and season with salt and black pepper as per your taste. Increase the flame to high and mix once again. When the specks of oil start to appear alongside pan walls, switch off the flame. The puree is ready.

  3. Boil pasta in salted water till al dente

    4. Add dried oregano to the pureed sauce. Meanwhile, take a deep-bottomed and non-stick pan over medium flame and add heat water in it. Add a little salt and bring water to a boil. After a boil, add penne pasta in the pan cook till al dente. Turn off the burner when done, and strain the pasta.

  4. Add strained pasta in the puree and garnish with basil leaves, serve hot

    5. Add pasta to the sauce and mix it well. Cook for a while until sauce coats the pasta well. When done, transfer the pasta to a serving plate and garnish with basil leaves and serve it hot.

Tips

  • Add red chilli flakes as per your own taste in the pasta as it should have a balanced flavour.
  • Use your favorite cheese as the topping for your pasta to increase the flavour.
